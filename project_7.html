Work

At Kuka Robotics, I gained valuable hands-on experience of industrial robotics. My responsibilities included the control and programming of industrial robots, emphasizing the significance of maintenance and safety protocols to ensure efficient and secure manufacturing processes. I also had the opportunity to implement PID control on a robotics arm, enhancing closed-loop control accuracy. Furthermore, I programmed and commissioned a KUKA ARM on a KRC controller, delving into the practical aspects of robotic operation. My internship also involved working with Kuka sim software for simulating work cells. I broaden my understanding at robot autonomy and robotic systems.
